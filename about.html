About Us

At Christ Apostolic Church Sanctuary of Praise, we are a Bible-believing, Spirit-filled community dedicated to worship, prayer, and the teaching of God’s Word. Rooted in the apostolic doctrine and the power of the Holy Spirit, our mission is to raise a generation of believers who are strong in faith, vibrant in praise, and passionate about the Kingdom of God. We are committed to spiritual growth, holiness, and the demonstration of God’s power through healing, deliverance, and prophetic ministry. At Sanctuary of Praise, every service is a divine encounter and every member is a vessel of God’s purpose. You are welcome to join us in glorifying God and transforming lives through His love and grace.
